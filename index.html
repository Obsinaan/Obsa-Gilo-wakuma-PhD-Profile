👤 About Me

Professional Background

I am a Post Doctoral Research Fellow at the Faculty of Engineering, North-West University (NWU), Mafikeng Campus, South Africa, specializing in advanced machine learning and deep learning research. Additionally, I serve as Head of the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at Wollega University, Ethiopia. With expertise in transfer learning, domain adaptation, and computer vision, I am dedicated to advancing cutting-edge research and fostering excellence in computer science education across Africa. My research bridges academic innovation with practical applications in real-world problem-solving.

Education & Experience

  • November 2025 – Present: Post Doctoral Research Fellow, Faculty of Engineering, North-West University (NWU), Mafikeng Campus, South Africa
  • May 2024 – November 2025: Assistant Professor and Head of CSE Department, Wollega University, Ethiopia

🔬 Research Interest

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Exploring advanced ML/DL algorithms and their applications in real-world problems with focus on efficiency and scalability.

Transfer Learning & Domain Adaptation

Developing efficient methods for knowledge transfer across different domains and datasets to reduce data requirements.

Computer Vision

Visual recognition, image processing, and deep learning models for vision tasks with applications in object detection and classification.

NLP for Low-Resource Languages

Natural Language Processing techniques tailored for Afaan Oromoo, Amharic, and other African languages.

Time Series Analysis & Forecasting

Advanced techniques for forecasting and pattern recognition in temporal data with applications in prediction and anomaly detection.

Cybersecurity & AI

Security vulnerabilities, threat detection using ML, and protective mechanisms leveraging artificial intelligence.
